Vegetable Stew

Meal: A comforting broth of healthy vegetables

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Potatos
  • Zucchini
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Water

Steps

  • Peel the carrots, potatoes, zucchini, and onion
  • Chop up all the vegetables
  • Store the chopped vegetables in a pot with some butter and water
  • Add some canned tomatoes, salt and pepper
  • Stir
  • Let the soup simmer for 30 minutes on the stove
  • Season (optional) and serve!

Extra Notes

  • Any vegetables can be used, as long as the stew is yummy!

Potstickers

Snack or part-meal: A delicious fried dumpling edition to an asian cuisine

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Pork
  • Won ton wrappers
  • Water (in a bowl)
  • Oil

Steps

  • Combine the chicken, onion, and pork in a mixing bowl (filling)
  • For each wrapper, add a teaspoon of filling onto the center of a wrapper
  • Still for every wrapper, dip your fingers into the water and fold the wrapper into a dumpling shape, enveloping the filling
  • Pour some oil onto the pan
  • Stir
  • Add one layer of potstickers onto the pan, and fry until golden crisp on the stove (2-3 minutes per side)
  • Season (optional) and serve!

Extra Notes

  • Don't overfill or overcook the potstickers!
  • Don't let the potstickers dry out before frying!
  • You can use clean hands to mix the filling!
