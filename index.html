Getting Started

Embark on your culinary journey NOW with WhiskIt!

Are you ready? ...

  • to whisk up a recipe with the power of AI at your side?
  • to mix your generated recipes with step-by-step AI images / videos?

  • to explore other chefs' recipes and gain inspiration for yours?
  • to publish your own recipe and inspire others?
  • or just to store and reuse your past recipes?

If you said YES, then

  • First continue to read through these instructions to ensure you know how to use WhiskIt!
  • Visit #Explore to view our recipes database!
  • Travel to #Whisk to generate a recipe with the help of AI!
  • Navigate to #Mix to generate an image (soon to be video) of each of the steps #Whisk provided you with!
  • Finally in #Publish, save your recipe to our database or view your previous recipes!

Api-Keys

  • We provide an AI chatbot for recipe generation and an AI bot for image generation. However, to use these two services, you must retrieve your own FREE api keys from groqcloud and stability.
  • Thank you for your understanding! We promise the process is extremely simple and is neccessary because of privacy concerns.
  • First, navigate to: https://console.groq.com/keys and sign in with your google account. Afterwards, please generate an api key and paste it in the Groq API key input box located in #Whisk before you generate a recipe.
  • Next, navigate to: https://platform.stability.ai/ and click login in the top right corner. Again, sign in with your google account. Afterwards, click your profile picture in the top right, which will redirect you to your API key dashboard. Copy the API key, and then paste it in the Stability AI input box in #Mix before you generate an image.
